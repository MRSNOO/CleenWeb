About Cleen Project

With the help of Summit Organization The Cleen Project was founded to bring clean stoves to rural villages. The project was founded by Nguyen Lan Chi and vice-president Nguyen Nhat Quang(Both are students of Hanoi Amsterdam High School) The aim of our project is to bring clean stoves to poor villages to replace the smoky and hazard ones and restore the enviroment of these places.

2019 hightlights

Minimize plastic bag and Against Water Pollution
Water Pollution, a contamination of bodies of water including lakes, rivers and oceans, has been the main causes of health related diseases in many countryside areas This year, thanks to the huge success of the project last year, we are going back to Phu Ly Ha Nam to help improve the water conditions and also spread our project to nearby villages We have received more support from the vllagers and we also partner with several institution and initiative to better our project. Our main goal is to drastically recover the heavily polluted environment in my countryside and expand our projects to more villages Stay tune for more upcoming events of our project!
